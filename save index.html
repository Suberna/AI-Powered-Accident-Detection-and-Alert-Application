Introduction

Problem Identified: Accidents often go unnoticed or are reported late, delaying emergency responses and risking lives.

Proposed Solution: GuardianX is an AI-powered mobile application that detects and classifies accidents by analyzing images and videos captured through the app. It provides detailed reports containing GPS coordinates and supporting media while offering offline functionality through SMS-based reporting.
